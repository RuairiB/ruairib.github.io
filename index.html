Research Interests:

Computational/theoretical particle physics, primarily first principles simulations of the strong interaction/QCD. Including, but not limited to:
  • lattice gauge theories
  • hadron/glueball spectroscopy
  • scattering from field theories in finite-volume
  • quantum computing for gauge theories
  • GPU parallelism in scientific computing
